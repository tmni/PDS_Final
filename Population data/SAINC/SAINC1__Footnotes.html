SAINC1 Footnotes
  • 1/ Census Bureau midyear population estimate. Estimates for 2010-2018 reflect Census Bureau midyear state population estimates available as of December 2018.
  • 2/ Per capita personal income is total personal income divided by total midyear population.
  • * Estimates prior to 1950 are not available for Alaska and Hawaii.
  • Note-- All dollar estimates are in millions of current dollars (not adjusted for inflation). Calculations are performed on unrounded data.
  • (L) Less than $50,000.
  • (NA) Not available.
  • (NM) Not meaningful.
